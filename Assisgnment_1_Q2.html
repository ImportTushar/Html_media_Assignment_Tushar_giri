comments are entities that are neglected by the compiler to be rendered on the display/rendering engine

Comments is used to potray something which is not promoted
to be shown on the rendering engine but it is mainly given to understand
the sequence/order/reason of the code.


Comments are usually starts with < ! -- and end with -- >


(Note between < !-- there is no gap)
